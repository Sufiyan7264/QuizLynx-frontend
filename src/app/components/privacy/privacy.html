1. Introduction

Welcome to QuizLynx. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Register for an account (name, email, password)
  • Create or join classrooms/batches
  • Take quizzes and assessments
  • Contact us for support

Automatically Collected Information

When you use QuizLynx, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)
  • Performance data (quiz scores, completion times)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process your quiz attempts and track progress
  • Enable classroom features and leaderboards
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Improve our platform based on usage patterns
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With Instructors: Students' quiz performance is visible to their instructors
  • Leaderboards: Public rankings display usernames and scores
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our platform

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Children's Privacy

QuizLynx is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
